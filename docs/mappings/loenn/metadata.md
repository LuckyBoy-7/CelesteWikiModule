* [Communal Helper](https://gamebanana.com/mods/53697), [Wiki](https://github.com/CommunalHelper/CommunalHelper/blob/db7b27060ebf64553e299fa1c98ab21534e360de/docs/Home.md#custom-textures): 改默认 Gem 材质
